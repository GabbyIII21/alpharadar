# Product Overview

Alpha Radar is an AI-powered crypto intelligence platform for traders, analysts, founders, and investors who need to understand fast-moving crypto events.

## Problem

Crypto markets generate too much fragmented information:

- Price and volume anomalies
- Whale movements
- Exchange inflows and outflows
- Funding and open interest changes
- Token unlocks
- Social narrative shifts
- News catalysts

Most dashboards show data but do not explain why the signal matters.

## Solution

Alpha Radar ranks meaningful events and produces concise AI reasoning:

- What happened
- Which asset is affected
- Whether the read is bullish, bearish, or neutral
- How urgent the signal is
- Which related signals support the conclusion
- What timeframe matters

## Demo Persona

The target demo user is a crypto analyst preparing for a market update. They need a reliable answer to what changed and where to focus next.

## Core User Flows

- Land on the product and understand the value proposition.
- Open the dashboard and scan current high-priority signals.
- Click an event and read the AI explanation.
- Ask Alpha follow-up questions.
- Monitor selected assets in the watchlist.
- Adjust alert and integration settings.
